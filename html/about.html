About the Site

This site was created as the last level of the application for Holberton School. The goal was to demonstrate my abilities in making a website entirely from scratch. I decided to make the subject of the website Lancaster County because it is the area I live in and it has many beautiful places and things to see combined with an intricate culture. My goal for the website was to make a simple, good-looking, informational site that was easy to navigate on both mobile and desktop. For anyone interested, the complete source code is available here.
